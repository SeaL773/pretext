Shape Playground

Blob Field

A grid of seeded polygon blobs. Nothing animates over time: every contour is a pure function of viewport width and height, so resizing the page makes the field warp like a frozen shader.

How to use it

Resize the window, squish the page, and look for families whose silhouettes stay interesting when the viewport aspect ratio changes. Then we can steal a winner back into the editorial demos.
